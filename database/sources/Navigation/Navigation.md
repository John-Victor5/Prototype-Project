# 🏛️ Navigation Guide — Baguio City Hall
### Public Information Office & Key Service Rooms

> Use this guide to locate offices, rooms, and service counters within **Baguio City Hall Compound**.
> For appointment scheduling, refer to [`Appointment.md`](./Appointment.md).

---

## 📍 Building Overview

**Address:** Baguio City Hall, Harrison Road, Baguio City, Benguet 2600
**Office Hours:** Monday – Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM (no noon break for frontline services)
**Main Entrance:** Harrison Road Gate (primary public entrance)
**Accessible Entrance:** Side ramp near the City Social Welfare Office

---

|---------------|----------|----------|

| **Comfort Rooms** | GF, Near elevator | — |
| **PWD / Senior Citizen Lane** | GF, All counters | Priority lane access |

## ♿ Accessibility Features

- **Ramp access** at the side entrance (near CSWDO Annex)
- **Elevator** available at GF lobby (limited capacity; priority for PWD & senior citizens)
- **Priority lanes** at all major service counters
- **Sign language assistance** — coordinate with PIO in advance

## 🧭 Getting to City Hall

- **By jeepney:** Ride routes passing Harrison Road (Burnham Park–SM, Central-Aurora Hill)
- **By taxi / Grab:** Drop-off at Harrison Road main gate
- **On foot:** 5-minute walk from Burnham Park or Session Road
