# 📋 Appointment System — Baguio City Public Information Office

> **Walk-ins are not entertained.** All clients must secure an appointment prior to visiting the office.

---

## 📅 Appointment Availability

| Field         | Details                                              |
|---------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| **Month**     | July (appointments open for the **following month** only; current month slots are unavailable) |
| **Days**      | Monday – Friday                                      |
| **Time Slots**| 8:00 AM – 12:00 PM · 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM              |
| **Venue**     | Baguio City Public Information Office, City Hall Compound, Baguio City |

> ⚠️ **Note:** You may only book appointments for the **next available month**. Same-day or current-month bookings are **not accepted**.

---

## 👤 Who May Request an Appointment?

The following individuals or entities may approach the Public Information Office:

- **City residents** with concerns on public records, permits, or city services
- **Business owners / entrepreneurs** with tax, licensing, or trade queries
- **Property owners / claimants** with land-related concerns
- **Media practitioners** requesting press releases, official statements, or accreditation
- **Students and researchers** needing official data or interviews with city officials
- **NGOs, civic groups, and barangay officials** coordinating with the city government
- **Other government agencies** on inter-agency coordination matters

| Code | Purpose Category                              |
|------|-----------------------------------------------|

## 📝 How to Book an Appointment

1. Identify your **concern category** from the table above.
2. Open the corresponding appointment file and review the **requirements**.
3. Contact the PIO through any of the following:
   - 📧 Email: `pio@baguio.gov.ph`
   - ☎️ Phone: (074) 300-0000 local 1250
   - 🌐 Online Form: [baguio.gov.ph/appointment](https://baguio.gov.ph/appointment) *(if available)*
4. Provide: **Full name, contact number, purpose, and preferred date/time slot.**
5. Wait for a **confirmation message** before visiting.

---

## ⚠️ Reminders

- Bring a **valid government-issued ID** on the day of appointment.
- Arrive **15 minutes before** your scheduled time.
- Failure to appear **twice without prior notice** will result in a **30-day booking restriction**.
- For rescheduling, notify the PIO at least **24 hours in advance**.
