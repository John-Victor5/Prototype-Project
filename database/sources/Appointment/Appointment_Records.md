# 📁 Appointment — Records Request & Document Certification
### Baguio City Public Information Office

> **Track Code:** `REC`
> **Parent file:** [`Appointment.md`](./Appointment.md)

---

## 📌 Who Should Use This Track?

Use this appointment type if you are:

- A **resident** requesting certified copies of official city documents
- A **legal representative or attorney** obtaining records for court or legal proceedings
- A **student or researcher** requesting public records for academic purposes
- A **business entity** needing certified government documents
- Any individual requesting records under the **Right to Information (RTI)** or **FOI** policy

---

## 📋 Requirements

### 📄 General Records Request
- [ ] **Written request letter** indicating: document name, purpose, and number of copies needed
- [ ] Valid **government-issued ID**
- [ ] **Authorization letter + ID of owner** (if requesting on behalf of another person)

### 📜 Certified True Copy (CTC) Request
- [ ] Identification of the specific document (ordinance number, resolution number, date, etc.)
- [ ] Valid ID
- [ ] Payment of **certification fee** (per city revenue code)

### 🔍 FOI / RTI Request
- [ ] Accomplished **FOI Request Form** (available at PIO or downloadable online)
- [ ] Valid ID
- [ ] Written justification of request

---

| Detail          | Info                                         |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Available Month | **July** *(book for the following month only)* |
| Days            | Monday – Friday                              |
| Time            | 8:00 AM – 12:00 PM · 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM     |
| Processing Time | 30 min – 5 business days (depending on document) |

---

## 🔄 Processing Flow

```
Client submits appointment + request letter
        ↓
PIO receives and logs the request
        ↓
Document is located and reviewed
        ↓
Certification / reproduction processed
        ↓
Client pays applicable fees (if any)
        ↓
Document released to client
```

---

## ⚠️ Important Reminders

- Some records may be **classified or restricted** and require additional clearances.
- **Voluminous requests** (10+ pages) may take longer — allow up to **5 business days**.
- Fees are based on the current **Baguio City Revenue Code**.
- Walk-ins **will not be entertained**.
