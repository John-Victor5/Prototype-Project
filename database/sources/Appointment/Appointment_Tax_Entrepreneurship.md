# 💼 Appointment — Tax & Entrepreneurship
### Baguio City Public Information Office

> **Track Code:** `TAX / ENT`
> **Parent file:** [`Appointment.md`](./Appointment.md)

---

## 📌 Who Should Use This Track?

Use this appointment type if you are:

- A **new business owner** applying for a Mayor's Permit or Business License
- An **existing business** renewing permits, paying business tax, or amending registrations
- A **taxpayer** with concerns on **Real Property Tax (RPT)** assessment or delinquency
- A business entity coordinating **BIR-related documents** through the city
- A **sole proprietor, partnership, corporation, or cooperative** operating within Baguio City

---

## 📋 Requirements Before Your Appointment

Prepare the following documents depending on your concern:

### 🏪 New Business Registration
- [ ] Duly accomplished **Business Permit Application Form**
- [ ] **DTI Registration** (sole proprietor) or **SEC / CDA Certificate** (corp/coop)
- [ ] **Barangay Clearance** (from the barangay where business is located)
- [ ] **Lease Contract** or proof of business address
- [ ] **Community Tax Certificate (CTC)** of the owner/representative
- [ ] Two (2) valid government-issued IDs

### 🔄 Business Permit Renewal
- [ ] Previous year's **Mayor's Permit**
- [ ] **Gross Sales / Income** declaration for the previous year
- [ ] **Official Receipt** of previous payments
- [ ] Updated **Barangay Clearance**

### 🏠 Real Property Tax (RPT)
- [ ] **Tax Declaration** of the property
- [ ] **Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT)** or **OCT**
- [ ] Valid ID of the taxpayer or authorized representative
- [ ] **Special Power of Attorney (SPA)** if transacting on behalf of the owner

### 📑 BIR Coordination / Other Tax Concerns
- [ ] Relevant **BIR documents or notices**
- [ ] Letter of intent or written query
- [ ] Valid ID

---

## 🔄 Processing Flow

```
Client submits appointment request (email/phone/online)
        ↓
PIO confirms slot and sends requirements checklist
        ↓
Client arrives with complete documents
        ↓
PIO staff receives and evaluates documents
        ↓
Referral to City Treasurer's Office / Business Permits & Licensing Office
        ↓
Processing / Assessment
        ↓
Release of permit, receipt, or official response
```

---

## 📞 Coordinating Offices

| Office | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **Business Permits & Licensing Office (BPLO)** | New & renewal of business permits |
| **City Treasurer's Office (CTO)** | Business tax, RPT, other city taxes |
| **City Assessor's Office** | Property valuation and tax declaration |
| **BIR – Baguio District Office** | National tax coordination |

---

## ⚠️ Important Reminders

- **Incomplete documents = no processing.** Prepare all requirements before visiting.
- Fees are subject to the current **Baguio City Revenue Code**.
- For large corporations or bulk transactions, request an **extended slot** when booking.
- Walk-ins **will not be entertained**.
