# 📰 Appointment — Media Relations & Press Coordination
### Baguio City Public Information Office

> **Track Code:** `MED`
> **Parent file:** [`Appointment.md`](./Appointment.md)

---

## 📌 Who Should Use This Track?

Use this appointment type if you are:

- A **journalist, reporter, or media practitioner** requesting an interview with city officials
- A **news organization** requesting official press releases or statements
- A **photographer or videographer** covering city events or government activities
- A student or researcher from **media/communications programs** seeking accreditation or access
- A **content creator or blogger** requesting official information for publication

---

## 📋 Requirements Before Your Appointment

### 🎤 Interview Request
- [ ] **Written interview request letter** (addressed to the City Mayor or Department Head)
- [ ] **Media ID** or press credential from your organization
- [ ] List of **interview questions** (submit at least 2 business days before)
- [ ] Purpose and intended **publication/broadcast outlet**

### 📋 Press Release / Official Statement Request
- [ ] **Written request letter** stating the topic and deadline
- [ ] Organization's **letterhead** or credential
- [ ] Contact information for follow-up

### 📷 Media Coverage / Accreditation
- [ ] **Application form** (available at the PIO front desk or via email)
- [ ] 2x2 ID photo (2 copies)
- [ ] Valid government ID
- [ ] Employer's **certificate or assignment letter**

---

## 🗓️ Appointment Slots

| Detail          | Info                                         |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Available Month | **July** *(book for the following month only)* |
| Days            | Monday – Friday                              |
| Time            | 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM · 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM *(media slots)* |
| Processing Time | 30 min – 1 hour                              |

---

## 🔄 Processing Flow

```
Media practitioner submits appointment request + credentials
        ↓
PIO reviews and endorses to the concerned official
        ↓
Confirmation of schedule sent to the practitioner
        ↓
Media practitioner arrives and presents credentials
        ↓
Interview / briefing / accreditation conducted
        ↓
PIO records and files the interaction
```

---

## ⚠️ Important Reminders

- All **interview schedules are subject to the availability** of the city official concerned.
- The PIO reserves the right to **decline or defer** requests that are not in the public interest or involve sensitive ongoing matters.
- Accreditation is valid for **one (1) calendar year** and must be renewed annually.
- Walk-ins **will not be entertained** for interview or accreditation requests.
