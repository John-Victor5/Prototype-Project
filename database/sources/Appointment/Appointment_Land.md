# 🗺️ Appointment — Land Concerns
### Baguio City Public Information Office

> **Track Code:** `LND`
> **Parent file:** [`Appointment.md`](./Appointment.md)

---

## 📌 Who Should Use This Track?

Use this appointment type if you are:

- A **property owner or heir** processing land titling or transfer
- A party involved in a **land dispute or boundary conflict**
- A client requesting **cadastral maps** or lot information
- A **developer or contractor** securing land-use clearance
- A buyer or seller needing **zoning verification**
- An individual with concerns on **Baguio City's Townsite Reservation** or ancestral land claims

---

## 📋 Requirements Before Your Appointment

Prepare the following depending on your land concern:

### 📜 Land Titling / Transfer of Title
- [ ] **Original Certificate of Title (OCT)** or **Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT)**
- [ ] **Deed of Sale, Donation, or Extrajudicial Settlement** (whichever applies)
- [ ] **Tax Declaration** of the property (latest)
- [ ] **Real Property Tax Clearance** (from City Treasurer's Office)
- [ ] Valid ID of all parties involved
- [ ] **BIR Certificate Authorizing Registration (CAR)**
- [ ] **Transfer Tax Receipt** from the City Treasurer

### 🔍 Cadastral Map / Lot Information Request
- [ ] **Written request letter** addressed to the City Assessor
- [ ] Known **lot number, block, or survey number**
- [ ] Valid ID of the requesting party
- [ ] Purpose of the request (research, legal, personal)

### ⚖️ Land Dispute / Boundary Concerns
- [ ] **Title or Tax Declaration** of the disputed property
- [ ] **Sketch plan or vicinity map** (if available)
- [ ] **Barangay Certification** of prior mediation (if applicable)
- [ ] Valid ID
- [ ] Written **statement of the dispute**

### 🏗️ Land Use / Zoning Clearance
- [ ] **Lot plan** signed by a licensed geodetic engineer
- [ ] **Title or Tax Declaration**
- [ ] **Application form** from the City Planning & Development Office
- [ ] Valid ID

## 🔄 Processing Flow

```
Client submits appointment request
        ↓
PIO confirms slot and sends requirements checklist
        ↓
Client arrives with complete documents
        ↓
PIO receives documents and verifies completeness
        ↓
Referral to appropriate office:
  ├── City Assessor's Office (tax declaration, cadastral)
  ├── City Treasurer's Office (tax clearance, transfer tax)
  ├── Register of Deeds (titling, transfer)
  ├── CPDO (zoning, land use)
  └── DILG / Barangay (dispute mediation)
        ↓
Processing / Assessment / Resolution
        ↓
Release of document, clearance, or official referral
```

---

## 📞 Coordinating Offices

| Office | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **City Assessor's Office** | Tax declaration, cadastral maps, property valuation |
| **City Treasurer's Office (CTO)** | RPT clearance, transfer tax |
| **Register of Deeds – Baguio** | Title registration and transfer |
| **City Planning & Development Office (CPDO)** | Zoning, land use permits |
| **DENR – Cordillera Region** | Ancestral land, forest land, CLOA |
| **Barangay / DILG** | Local dispute mediation |

---

## ⚠️ Important Reminders

- **Land disputes involving court orders** must present the relevant court document.
- **Ancestral land claims** under IPRA must be coordinated with the **National Commission on Indigenous Peoples (NCIP)**.
- Baguio City has unique land policies under **CA 141 (Townsite Reservation)**; consult the City Legal Office for complex cases.
- Walk-ins **will not be entertained**.
